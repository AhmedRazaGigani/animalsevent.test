Motor City Jazz Club / Midwest Persian Tabby Fanciers

Event Series (See All)

July 22, 2028 All day

Welcome to the Motor City Jazz Club, an extraordinary event that combines the world of jazz music with a delightful showcase of feline wonders. As an avid animal lover and visitor to this incredible event, I can confidently say that it offers an unparalleled experience for cat parents, cat lovers, and anyone fascinated by these magnificent creatures. From the diverse range of cat breeds to captivating competitions and educational talks, the Motor City Jazz Club is a must-visit for families and individuals alike.

I. An Array of Cat Breeds:

Step into the Motor City Jazz Club, and you'll find yourself immersed in a world of diverse feline breeds. From regal Persians to energetic Siamese and playful Maine Coons, this event presents an opportunity to marvel at the beauty and uniqueness of various cat breeds. Whether you're a seasoned cat enthusiast or new to the world of felines, the Motor City Jazz Club ensures a visual feast of captivating cat breeds.

II. Beyond Cats: Exotic Animals and More:

While the focus of the Motor City Jazz Club revolves around cats, the event also provides a platform to appreciate and learn about other exotic animals. It's not just a cat lover's paradise but an animal lover's haven. Visitors can encounter a range of exotic animals, further enhancing their experience and making the event an all-encompassing affair.

III. Catering to Every Need: Cage Supplies and Feeders:

At the Motor City Jazz Club, you'll find a wide array of exhibitor booths offering cage supplies and feeders to meet your feline companion's every need. From comfortable beds and scratching posts to interactive toys and premium cat food, these vendors ensure that your beloved feline receives the best care and attention possible.

IV. Judging and Competitions:

Witness the excitement unfold as experienced judges assess the beauty, grace, and unique traits of the participating cats. The judging and competitions at the Motor City Jazz Club add a competitive edge to the event, showcasing the finest examples of each breed. Cheer on your favorite felines as they compete for prestigious titles and awards.

V. Demonstrations of Grooming, Training, and Behavior:

The Motor City Jazz Club goes beyond the visual appeal of cats by offering informative demonstrations on grooming, training, and behavior. Experienced professionals share their expertise and provide valuable insights into maintaining your feline companion's health, happiness, and well-being. Learn tips and tricks to keep your cat looking and feeling its best.

VI. Cat Agility Competitions:

Prepare to be amazed as you witness the agility competitions at the Motor City Jazz Club. Cats gracefully navigate through a series of obstacles, showcasing their intelligence, agility, and innate abilities. These thrilling competitions captivate audiences, demonstrating the remarkable capabilities of our feline friends.

VII. Cat Rescue and Adoption:

The Motor City Jazz Club is not just about celebrating cats; it's also a platform for cat rescue and adoption. Engage with various rescue organizations and learn about the rewarding experience of providing a loving home to a rescue cat. Find your new furry friend and make a positive impact in a cat's life.

VIII. Educational Talks and Seminars:

Expand your knowledge and gain valuable insights through educational talks and seminars presented by renowned experts. Topics range from cat health and nutrition to behavior and training, ensuring that visitors leave the Motor City Jazz Club with a deeper understanding of feline care and well-being.

IX. Cat-related Merchandise:

Indulge in a shopping spree at the Motor City Jazz Club's cat-related merchandise booths. Discover an extensive range of products, including toys, accessories, grooming tools, and unique cat-themed items. Take home a special memento to commemorate your visit or find the perfect gift for the cat lover in your life. With a plethora of options available, you'll find something to suit every taste and budget.

X. Socializing and Networking with Other Cat Parents and Cat Lovers:

The Motor City Jazz Club provides a wonderful opportunity to connect and network with like-minded individuals who share a deep love for cats. Engage in conversations, exchange experiences, and forge new friendships with fellow cat parents and enthusiasts. This event creates a sense of community, where you can bond over your shared passion for these magnificent creatures.

XI. Cat FOR SALE:

For those looking to add a new member to their feline family, the Motor City Jazz Club offers a unique chance to find the perfect cat for sale. Reputable breeders and sellers showcase their available cats, allowing you to meet potential companions and make an informed decision about your next furry family member. Adopting a cat from this trusted event ensures that you bring home a healthy and well-cared-for feline friend.

XII. Convincing Cat Parents, Cat Owners, and Cat Lovers to Attend:

To all the cat parents, owners, and lovers out there, I wholeheartedly recommend attending the Motor City Jazz Club. This one-of-a-kind event combines entertainment, education, and the sheer joy of being in the company of these incredible animals. Whether you want to learn more about cat care, enjoy the agility competitions, or simply revel in the beauty of various breeds, the Motor City Jazz Club caters to your every desire.

Bring your kids along, as the event offers a family-friendly environment where children can learn about responsible pet ownership and develop a deeper appreciation for cats. The Motor City Jazz Club guarantees an unforgettable experience that will leave a lasting impression on everyone involved.

Conclusion:

As someone who has personally experienced the wonders of the Motor City Jazz Club, I can confidently say that it is a haven for cat parents, cat lovers, and anyone who appreciates the beauty and charm of these extraordinary creatures. From the diverse array of cat breeds and exotic animals to the engaging competitions, educational talks, and cat-related merchandise, this event offers something for everyone.

Make plans to visit the Motor City Jazz Club and immerse yourself in a world where cats reign supreme. Come and witness the grace, agility, and captivating presence of these incredible felines. Don't miss out on this extraordinary opportunity to connect with fellow cat enthusiasts, learn from experts, and celebrate the profound bond we share with our feline companions. Mark your calendars and prepare for an unforgettable experience at the Motor City Jazz Club.
