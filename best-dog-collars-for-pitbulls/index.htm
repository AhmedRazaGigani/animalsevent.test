Crafted from durable nylon, this dog collar boasts thick neoprene padding on the back. This not only adds to its durability but also enhances comfort. The soft padding prevents any irritation on the neck, making it an excellent choice for dogs with sensitive skin. The Joytale Reflective Dog Collar strikes a perfect balance between classic design and contemporary style. With 12 color options and 5 sizes to choose from, you can find the perfect collar to suit your Pitbull's personality. For quick and hassle-free use, the buckles on this collar are made of durable ABS plastic. The quick-release buckles allow for easy adjustment around your dog's neck, ensuring a snug and comfortable fit. The collar is suitable for dogs of all stages, from puppies to large breeds, providing a versatile and long-lasting accessory.
